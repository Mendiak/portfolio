About Me

Hi! I'm Mikel. I live in El Maresme, Barcelona province.

I enjoy experimenting with code, design, and new tools.

While I'm not a full-time web developer, I take pride in crafting sites that are intuitive and enjoyable to use.

The Technologies I Use

In my web projects, I focus on crafting experiences that feel clear, engaging, and purposeful.

My process blends visual design, interaction, and usability.

I also hold the Google UX Design Certificate, which has deepened my understanding of user-centered methodologies and accessible design.
